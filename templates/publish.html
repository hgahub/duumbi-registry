Publishing to the registry

How to publish your duumbi modules for others to use.

Configure a registry

Add the registry URL to your workspace configuration.

duumbi registry add myregistry https://registry.example.com

Authenticate

Log in with your API token.

duumbi registry login myregistry --token duu_your_token_here

Package your module

Ensure your module has a manifest.toml with name, version, and description.

duumbi package

Publish

Push your package to the registry.

duumbi publish --registry myregistry
